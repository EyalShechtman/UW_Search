SCHOOL OF MEDICINE
DERMATOLOGY

Detailed course offerings (Time Schedule) are available for

  • Autumn Quarter 2024
  • Winter Quarter 2025

DERM 499 Undergraduate Research (1-10, max. 25)
Laboratory research within the Department of Dermatology. Must be supervised directly by UW faculty. Credit/no-credit only. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 499

DERM 604 P-Advanced Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Seattle, WA (8)
Clinical clerkship. Participation in dermatology clinics and inpatient consultations. Weekly clinical conferences with the entire department as well as a continuing series of teaching seminars and dermatopathology conferences. (Four weeks). Prerequisite: third-year required Internal Medicine clerkship. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 604

DERM 634 Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Spokane, WA (4/8, max. 8)
Clerkship with participation in dermatology clinics and inpatient consultations to gain experience and confidence in description of skin findings and perform special dermatologic diagnostic procedures. Prerequisite: third-year required Medicine clerkship. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 634

DERM 654 Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Alaska (8)
Clinical clerkship. Participation in approach, diagnosis, and treatment of dermatological issues in outpatient dermatology clinics, as well as a continuing series of teaching seminars. (Four weeks). Prerequisite: third-year required Medicine clerkship. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 654

DERM 664 Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Billings, MT (8)
Participation in dermatology clinics and inpatient consultations. Includes a continuing series of teaching seminars, a dermatopathology conference, and a journal club. (Four weeks). Prerequisite: third-year required Medicine clerkship. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 664

DERM 674 Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Idaho (8)
Clinical clerkship. Participation in dermatology clinics, as well as a continuing series of teaching seminars. (Four weeks). Prerequisite: third-year required Medicine clerkship. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 674

DERM 697 Dermatology Special Electives - Away (2-24, max. 24)
Special clerkship or externship opportunities made available at institutions other than University of Washington outside of the Washington, Wyoming, Montana, Alaska, Idaho (WWAMI) region. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 697

DERM 699 Dermatology Special Electives - WWAMI (2-24, max. 24)
By special arrangement for qualified students, special clerkships or externships may be available at institutions, other than the University of Washington, located within the Washington, Wyoming, Alaska, Montana, Idaho (WWAMI) region. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 699

DERM 744 Introduction to Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Sheridan, WY (4)
Participation in patient care in a busy rural dermatology practice under the direction of a board-certified dermatologist, experienced physician assistant (PA), or nurse practitioner (NP). Introduction to the role of dermatology in healthcare, including diagnosis and management of common skin cancers and conditions. (Two weeks). Prerequisite: completion of Foundations curriculum. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 744

DERM 764 Introduction to Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Bozeman, MT (4/8, max. 8)
Clinical clerkship. Introduction to a variety of general-dermatologic clinic exposures and patient care, including surgical assist for cancer procedures. Shared time spent between surgical and general dermatology. Prerequisite: successful completion of Internal Medicine clerkship. Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 764

DERM 774 Introduction to Clinical Dermatology Clerkship - Boise, ID (4)
Clinical rotation designed to help students develop a basic approach to the diagnosis and management of common skin conditions. (Two weeks). Offered: AWSpS.
View course details in MyPlan: DERM 774
