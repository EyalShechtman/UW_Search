PUBLIC POLICY AND GOVERNANCE

  • Winter Quarter 2025

PA EX 501 Strategic Leadership I (5)
Explores public leadership at personal and institutional level. Examines institutional leadership in organizations, teams, and communities as well as personal leadership development.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 501

PA EX 502 Organizational Leadership I (2-6, max. 6)
Examines essential concepts and skills to lead and manage organizations in public service.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 502

PA EX 503 Policy Leadership I (2-6, max. 6)
Examines settings and processes for making public policies through fields of politics, economics, communications, policy studies, and related disciplines. Introduces core principles and tools of economic and behavioral analysis.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 503

PA EX 504 Policy Leadership II (4)
Introduces concepts, theories, frameworks, and practice to conduct policy analysis and communicate assessments to intended audiences.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 504

PA EX 505 Organizational Leadership II (4)
Introduces concepts and skills to assess and redesign implementation of public policies and programs. Examines use of participatory tools for analyzing and redesigning services, work flows, and delivery systems.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 505

PA EX 506 Policy Leadership III (2-6, max. 6)
Provides concepts and skills to assess and advance a policy proposal's prospects for successful enactment and implementation. Examines legislative relations, budget processes, policy field analysis, and negotiations to practice tools for understanding and increasing the viability of policy recommendations.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 506

PA EX 507 Organizational Leadership III (2-6, max. 6)
Provides concepts and skills to measure, evaluate, and improve the performance of policies, programs, and organizations. Considers public value through the lenses of performance management and equity.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 507

PA EX 508 Strategic Leadership II (2)
Focuses on crafting strategies to leading change as well as building changes into organizational culture.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 508

PA EX 509 Leading Across Boundaries (3)
Examines the modern challenges of leading across complex networks of institutions and stakeholders, examining institutional, national, sectoral, and cultural boundaries. Examines these challenges in the context of globalization and the emergence of multi-sectoral and multi-cultural networks of governance.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 509

PA EX 510 Strategic Negotiations (4)
Explores a theoretical framework for negotiations with an equal emphasis on developing negotiation skills through role-plays. Focuses on techniques and nuances of negotiation that are most useful for a manager working in a complex, dynamic environment with multiple stakeholders.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 510

PA EX 511 Strategic Communication (3)
Focuses on effective communication, highlighting the advocacy and motivation facet of leadership. In order to understand elements of persuasive communication, emphasizes concepts of negotiation analysis; power and dependence, perceptions of conflict, and finding mutual gains.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 511

PA EX 512 Strategic Policy-Making (4)
Examines how managers can expertly function at the intersection of politics, policy, and the wishes of the public.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 512

PA EX 513 Leading for a Legacy (3)
Explores the nature of human culture and values and how they relate to human legacy by impacting the quality of humans in an organization and how legacy grows from the values of the trusteeship and stewardship.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 513

PA EX 548 Leadership Salon (1)
Provides opportunity and structure for students to engage in reflection on leadership growth.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 548

PA EX 549 Racial Equity Skills Lab (1)
Provides opportunity and structure for student reflection on racial equity and equity literacy.
View course details in MyPlan: PA EX 549
