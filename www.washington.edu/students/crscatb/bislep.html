BISLEP 301 Law, Economics, and Public Policy (5) SSc
Examines the relationships among the fields of law, economics, politics, and public policy, with particular attention to problems of social, economic, and political change. Uses examples from various areas of public policy, including social, environmental, and education policy. Course overlaps with: TECON 350. Prerequisite: ECON 200, B CUSP 200, BIS 200, or BBUS 220.
View course details in MyPlan: BISLEP 301

BISLEP 302 Policy Analysis (5) SSc
Provides an introduction to the approaches and methods used in analysis of laws and policy utilizing case studies, statistics, and demographic evidence. Draws on contribution from microeconomics, statistics, political analysis, and social demography to examine trade-offs make in public policy design and implementation as well as impacts of implemented polices.
