Post categories: Announcements

Resources from the REU and Summer Research Workshop 

This workshop provided valuable insights into Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) programs and other summer research opportunities. This session covered:

  • What REUs and summer research programs are 
  • How to identify programs that align with your goals
  • Key application tips (e.g., personal statements, letters of recommendation)
  • Resources for crafting a strong application
