UW-IT Accessible Technology Services

UW-ITâ€™s Accessible Technology Services (ATS) provides services designed to increase the accessibility of IT developed, procured and used at the University of Washington. ATS staff includes several globally renowned experts in the field of technology accessibility. Although staff members have specialty areas as listed below, there’s a lot of overlap in the work they provide. For a list of services offered by ATS, and information about how to receive help, see Getting Help with Accessibility.
