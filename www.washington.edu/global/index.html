A Global University

At the University of Washington, we educate 50,000 students annually, and the Husky community is connected around the globe. Thanks to our incredible faculty and researchers, we yield a world of opportunity for students such as study abroad, research, internships, and classroom experiential learning. We take pride in our global impact which touches communities locally, nationally and internationally.

Welcome from University of Washington President Ana Mari Cauce

As one of the world’s most innovative universities, we are proud to deliver on our mission of education, discovery and public service – in Washington and across every continent.
