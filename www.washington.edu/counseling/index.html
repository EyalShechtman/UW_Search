Mental Health

The Counseling Center is here to connect you to the care you need.

As a UW student, your college experience should be challenging but not overwhelming. The UW Counseling Center is here to support you during your time at UW so that you have a positive, fulfilling experience.

In a life-threatening emergency, always call 911.

Are you in crisis?

If you are in crisis and need to talk to someone, Husky HelpLine operated by Telus (formally MySSP) to give students access to same-day, confidential mental health and crisis intervention support, 24/7 and in multiple languages.

Husky HelpLine is available to support you to same-day access to a Licensed Mental Health Counselor.

There are several ways to connect: 

  • Phone: (206) 616-7777
  • Online chat: Chat with a Telus counselor on Telus website or on the Telus app (Apple App Store | Google Play) 
  • Download the Telus app (Apple App Store | Google Play) to have the full range of services at your fingertips any time.

Phone – 206.543.1240 Address – 401 Schmitz Hall
