Board of deans and chancellors

The board of deans and chancellors considers matters of special interest related to educational policy, administrative processes and operations under the presidential orders, the faculty code and governance, the student governance and policies, and the employment and administrative policies. The board is co-chaired by the provost and one elected member of the board. Anind Dey, dean of the Information School, is the current chair of the board of deans and chancellors.
