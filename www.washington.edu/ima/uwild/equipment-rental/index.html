Availability and Reserving Gear

Reservation Process

  • A reservation request can be madeonline.The submission of this inquiry form is not a completed reservation. All inquiries must be finalized with a Gear Garage Staff person.
  • A credit card is required for the security deposit.
  • We accept reservation requests for up to a month in advance.
  • We may not be able to confirm reservation requests submitted within 3-days of pick up.
  • Confirmed reservations must be picked up at the day and time confirmed during the reservation process. Failure to do so will result in a penalty.

Cancellation Policy

  • Confirmed reservations that are not picked byon the specified day are canceled

Rental Policies & Procedures

Who May Rent?

  • Enrolled Seattle campus students with valid Husky Card. Duringthe quarter, students must be registered inclasses to receive the student rate.
  • Groups including RSOs, UW departments, fraternities, and sororities will be charged thestandard rate.
  • All UW Employees are eligible to rent, Rec membership not required. Employment verification may be required at the time of pick up. Contact us for more details.
  • Plus Ones, Alumni, Bothell & Tacoma students must possess an active Rec Membership to be eligible.

Pricing Structure & Student Discount

  • How is it we charge less than other rental stores? We believe that the UW Community is responsible and will treat our equipment as if it was borrowed from a close friend. We do not build in the cost of damages, late fees, cleaning costs, etc, into our rental rate as many other stores do. These charges are applied situationally. The vast majority of people renting from us incur no additional costs.
  • There is no charge for the day equipment is picked up and the day it’s dropped off.
  • The student discount is not transferrable to others, nor can it be applied to multiples of the same item.
  • Prices are subject to change.

Deposits, Fees, & Fines

  • All equipment must be returned clean and dry. Items in need of cleaning and/or drying are charged a fee of $20 per item.
  • Damaged equipment is charged a repair fee based on the materials and labor needed to repair the item.
  • Equipment returned late is chargedthe standard first-day rateof each item, for each day, the equipment is overdue.
  • Lost items are charged the retail value of the item including tax and shipping.

Off-Hours Appointments

To schedule an appointment, please contact the Gear Garage at recgear@uw.edu or 206.685.6767 Off-hours pick up and drop off are subject to availability. Off-hours pick up is subject to a convenience fee.

Wilderness Educator & Adventure Leadership program

UWild Adventures’ Wilderness Educator program is the first step on the trail to becoming a trip leader and instructor.

This course covers essential skills that every wilderness instructor, guide, and trip leader must develop:

  • backcountry decision-making,
  • risk mitigation and management,
  • leave no trace ethics,
  • group development and management,
  • sporting and living skills.

Questions About Accessibility?

UW Recreations programs, operations, and facilities are intended to be inclusive and accessible. If at any time, you have a need or concern please contact us, we encourage everyone to participate in our department offerings. For accommodation requests connected to a disability or health condition contact us at(206) 543-4590or atrecinfo@uw.edu. Requests can be responded to most effectively if received as far in advance as possible, preferably at least 10 business days.
