Rec on Demand

UW Recreation’s Rec on Demand program gives students, staff, and faculty members an opportunity to create and participate in customizable fitness and/or mindfulness experiences. These uniquely crafted classes and/or workshops are geared to support programming in residence halls, fraternities/sororities, Registered Student Organizations, academic classes, departments/offices, and other organized groups.

If you require a space to hold your event, you can check out info about facility reservations at the IMA or at the HUB. Students and/or student groups receive free space rental at the IMA with their Rec on Demand request.

Apply now!

Pricing + Logistics:

  • Student group pricing is $55 for an hour class and $110 for an hour workshop
  • Department pricing is $70 for an hour class and $140 for an hour workshop
    • Groups over 30 people require a second instructor for double the price
    • 75-minute class rates are $68.75 for student groups and $87.50 for departments
    • 90-minute class rates are $82.50 for student groups and $120 for departments
    • Additional time due to travel off-campus will be billed at the ¼ hour
    • Please give us 3-4 weeks lead time to process and assign your request to an instructor.
    • If you need to cancel an event, it must be done with 24 hours’ notice.

Payment Information:

  • Students can pay online via our web portal or UW budget transfer if applicable
  • Departments are requested to pay via UW budget transfer
  • You will be contacted by our department to coordinate payment options and details
