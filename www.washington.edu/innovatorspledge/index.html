  • Innovator's Pledge

As a powerhouse of change, the UW fuels the region’s and the world’s innovation ecosystem by educating tomorrow’s trailblazers, inspiring creativity and entrepreneurship, shaping the future and embodying the limitless spirit of the Pacific Northwest.

This innovation mindset influences discovery, brings invention to market and turns ideas into impact.

As a change creator yourself, you can join us in this pursuit. By making a personal, nonbinding pledge, you commit to reinvesting your success back into the UW. When you’re ready, you can support the Innovation Fund, which bolsters University programs and practices that advance entrepreneurs and their projects to the commercialization stage. Or you may direct a gift to a specific school or program that matters to you.

Fuel discovery and entrepreneurship.
