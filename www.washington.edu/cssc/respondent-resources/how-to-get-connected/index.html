How to Get Connected

Student Respondents can connect with a Respondent Resource based on the type of proceeding they are named in.

  • Title IX-related proceedings: If you are alleged to have violated Chapter 210 of the Student Governance and Policies (Student Conduct Policy for Discriminatory and Sexual Harassment, Intimate Partner Violence, Sexual Misconduct, Stalking, and Retaliation) your Title IX case manager will email you with more information. You can also fill out this request form to get support sooner.  
  • Student Conduct proceedings: If you are alleged to have violated Chapter 209 of the Student Governance and Policies (Student Conduct Policy for Academic Misconduct and Behavioral Misconduct) and your case is converted to a full hearing, you will automatically be referred to a Respondent Resource who will help you prepare.
  • Administrative Review process: You may request a Respondent Resource on your own at any time in the process, including cases in which you are thinking about an administrative review. To speak with someone about working with a Respondent Resource, please contact cssc@uw.edu.
