Rosa Ramirez named assistant dean of finance and personnel

Undergraduate Academic Affairs

Undergraduate Academic Affairs is pleased to announce Rosa Ramirez’s new role as assistant dean of finance and personnel.

Rosa Ramirez, assistant dean for finance and administration in Undergraduate Academic Affairs.Photo by Jayden Becles

Ramirez has dedicated over 20 years to serving in higher education, with her most recent role being acting assistant dean of finance and personnel, following a tenure as director of human resources and interim director of finance at UAA.

Ramirez’s leadership includes a rich history of strategic partnership experience and change management. “We are delighted that Rosa will bring her expertise in leading all financial and operational aspects of Undergraduate Academic Affairs,” said Ed Taylor, vice provost and dean of UAA. “Her extensive experience in overseeing finance and budget, human resources and facilities functions will be invaluable, and she plays a crucial role in UAA’s operations and governance,” he said.

Congratulations, Rosa Ramirez!
