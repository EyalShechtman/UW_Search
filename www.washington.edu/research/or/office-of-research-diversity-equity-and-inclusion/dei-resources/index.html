The University of Washington has many diversity, equity and inclusion resources available to staff, students and faculty. These resources can be found across several websites from UW Human Resources to the Office of Minority Affairs and Diversity. The DEI Resource tool provides a quick and easy way to look up these resources by topic or category.

Report all non-emergency incidents of bias or suspected bias using the UW's bias reporting tool, which was created in partnership with the Office of Minority Affairs and Diversity, the Office of Student Life and the president's Race & Equity Initiative. Reports can be anonymous. Submission of a Bias Incident Report does not constitute a formal complaint and will not automatically initiate an investigation.
