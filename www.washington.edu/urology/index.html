About us

Urology is a specialty that deals with diseases of the male and female urinary tract (kidneys, ureters, bladder and urethra). It also deals with the male reproductive organs (penis, testes, scrotum, prostate, etc.).

Learn more

Patient care

Urology is proud to serve our community at UW Medicine, Seattle Children's and partner locations throughout the greater Seattle area. Our experts see patients with a wide range of urological conditions at the top hospitals in the region.
