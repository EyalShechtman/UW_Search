]]> Comment on Announcement of the Preferred Candidate for UW Provost by Ira Kalet https://www.washington.edu/president/2011/11/17/announcement-of-the-preferred-candidate-for-uw-provost/#comment-207 Fri, 18 Nov 2011 21:10:16 +0000 http://depts.washington.edu/presblog/?p=834#comment-207 Dear President Young,

I have served on the faculty of the UW for over 30 years, and seen many presidents and provosts come and go. I count President Emeritus Lee Huntsman among my friends; others I have known less well. I add my thoughts to those of the many who have already spoken out.

I strongly support the selection of Ana Mari Cauce as our next Provost. Even a national search would not likely find a better qualified person than Ana Mari.

This choice, though excellent, and the minimal process you have added at the last minute, in no way mitigates the truly gross disrespect you have shown the UW faculty in the conduct of the final stages of the search process. I strongly urge you to take every opportunity going forward to carefully follow and reinforce the UW administration’s commitment to the principles of shared governance and respect for the Faculty Code, along with other fundamental principles on which this university has become one of the greatest in our nation and in the world. You, not Ana Mari, must take the lead on this, though past experience suggests that she can provide you much good insight from her years of successful experience here.

You have a huge opportunity still before you. More than ever, we need you to help keep the UW strong and focused on our mission of researh, teaching and service, in the face of budgetary and other attacks. If you don’t do it, who will? If not now, when?
