Veterans Education Benefits Office

We are available via email at veteran@uw.edu. We are available for drop in advising during our in-person advising hours in Room 520, Schmitz Hall.

* Starting October 7th, in-person advising is temporarily moved to Schmitz Hall room 105.

Office Hours

In-person advising:

Monday – Wednesday 1 p.m. – 3 p.m.

December 16th through January 3rd:

In-person advising is unavailable as we prepare for the winter quarter.
You can reach us via email or leave a voice message for call back. We can set up zoom appointments upon request.

Starting January 6th:

Monday – Friday 1 p.m. – 3 p.m.
