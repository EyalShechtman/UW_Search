School and Departmental Ceremonies

In addition to the official Commencement ceremony at Husky Stadium, there are over 100 school, college, departmental, and student organization graduation celebrations. Most take place during the week before Commencement or on the morning of Commencement Day. Links to event pages for each of schools, departments, or groups listed below will be added as information becomes available. Information for all ceremonies is summarized in our 2024 At-A-Glance Guide.

If you need immediate information about a school, college or departmental ceremony, please contact them directly or use the contact info in the listing below.
